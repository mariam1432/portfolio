About Me

I’m a front-end developer with a focus on building clean, responsive, and accessible web applications.
I’ve worked with React.js, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, and a bit of PHP and MySQL. I’ve also had hands-on experience with React Native during my time at my previous job, where I contributed to real-world mobile apps. These days, I’m diving deeper into Next.js to expand my skills around performance, routing, and server-side rendering.
I enjoy turning ideas into smooth, interactive user interfaces — whether it’s integrating APIs, organizing reusable components, or fine-tuning layouts to look great on every screen. I like writing code that’s not just functional but also easy to read and maintain.
Outside of development, I’m working in a corporate banking role, which strengthened my eye for detail, discipline, and patience traits I carry into every project I work on. I’m always learning, always building, and always up for a good challenge.
